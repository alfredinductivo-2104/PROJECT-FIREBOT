## OVERVIEW

The RC Fire Fighting Robot is a mobile robotic system controlled through an HTML-based controller, allowing the user to remotely control the robot. The system is designed primarily for fire detection. Once a presence of fire is detected, the DC water pump is activated to spray water, while a servo motor adjusts the direction of the water hose to effectively target the fire source.

In addition to fire detection, the system is equipped with a temperature and humidity sensor to monitor environmental conditions. The measured temperature and humidity values are displayed in real time on an LCD screen. Based on these readings, the system classifies it into different warning levels such as caution, extreme caution, or danger. Corresponding LED indicators are used to visually alert the user about the current heat condition, helping enhance safety awareness during operation.

## MATERIALS
- 4 Pin KY-026 Flame Sensor
- Temperature and Humidity Sensor (DT11)
- ESP32 Wifi & Bluetooth Development Board
- Arduino Nano
- 5V Relay – 1 channel
- DC Small Water Pump
- Servo Motor (MG996R Tower Pro)
- DC Motors (JGA25-370 12V 280RPM)
- Light Indicator (Traffic Light LED Module)
- 16 x 2 LCD 1602A with I2C
- Jumper Wires
- 12 V Battery
- 5 V Battery (2pcs)
- Cellphone
- Acrylic for Casing
